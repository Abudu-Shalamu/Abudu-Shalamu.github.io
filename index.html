Chang Hasheminezhad-Li

PhD Researcher in Inclusion, Well-Being and Sustainability in Education

I am a doctoral researcher at the University of Padova, specializing in children's literature, multimodal analysis, and representations of uncertainty.

My current research, "Hope as Cultural Resource: A Multimodal Analysis of Uncertainty Navigation in European Picturebooks (2015-2025)," employs multimodal analysis to explore how uncertainty is navigated in European picturebooks. I work to bridge the gap between literary theory and social impact, focusing on digital censorship and cross-cultural narratives.

I also have a strong interest in the relationship between multimodality, children's agency, and censorship.
