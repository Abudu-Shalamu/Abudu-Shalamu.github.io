Chang Hasheminezhad-Li

PhD Researcher in Children's Literature & Multimodality

I am a doctoral researcher at the University of Padova, specializing in the intersection of inclusion, political identity, and youth engagement within children's literature.

My current research, "Hope as Cultural Resource," employs multimodal analysis to explore how uncertainty is navigated in European picturebooks. I work to bridge the gap between literary theory and social impact, focusing on digital censorship and cross-cultural narratives.
